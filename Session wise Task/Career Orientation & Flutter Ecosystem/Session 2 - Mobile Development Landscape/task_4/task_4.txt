Given the following widget tree: Scaffold > Column > [ProfilePic, Username, FollowersRow],
draw or describe the widget hierarchy for a BookMyShow-like movie ticket card, including at least four widgets.

ans. 

Scaffold
└── Center
    └── Card
        └── Padding
            └── Column
                ├── Image (Movie Poster)
                ├── Text (Movie Name)
                ├── Row
                │   ├── Icon (Location)
                │   └── Text (Theater Name)
                ├── Row
                │   ├── Icon (Calendar)
                │   ├── Text (Date)
                │   └── Text (Time)
                └── ElevatedButton (Book Tickets)

Widgets used:
1. Scaffold 	   – Provides the basic app layout.
2. Center 	   – Centers the card on the screen.
3. Card 	   – Displays the movie ticket information in a card.
4. Padding 	   – Adds spacing inside the card.
5. Column 	   – Arranges the card contents vertically.
6. Image 	   – Displays the movie poster.
7. Text 	   – Shows the movie name, theater, date, and time.
8. Row 		   – Places icons and related text horizontally.
9. Icon 	   – Represents location and calendar visually.
10. ElevatedButton – Allows the user to book tickets.
