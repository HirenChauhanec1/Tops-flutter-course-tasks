App Chosen: Instagram

Feature Request 
Feature Name: Scheduled Posts for Personal Accounts

Description:
As a user, I want to schedule my posts to be published automatically at a specific date and time, so I don't have to be online when I want the post to go live. This feature would be useful for students, creators, and anyone who wants to plan their content in advance.

Team Members and Their Responsibilities

1.Product Manager 	    : Defines the feature requirements, prioritizes it in the product roadmap, and coordinates work across the team.
2.Designer		        : Creates the user interface for selecting the date and time, designs the scheduling flow, and ensures it is easy to
                          use.
3.Backend/API Developer	: Builds APIs to store scheduled posts, manages the scheduling service, and ensures posts are published automatically
                          at the correct time.
4.Mobile Developer	    : Implements the scheduling screens in the app, connects them to the backend APIs, and displays the scheduled posts to
                          users.
5.Tester (QA Engineer)  : Verifies that users can schedule, edit, and cancel posts, checks that posts are published at the correct time, and
                          tests edge cases such as network failures and different time zones.
